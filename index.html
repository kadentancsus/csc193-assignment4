2G Mitsubishi Eclipse

One car I find really interesting is the 2nd generation Mitsubishi Eclipse (1995-1999), especially the turbo AWD GSX model. The 4G63 turbo engine is well known in tuner culture because it responds well to upgrades and has strong aftermarket support. I like the mix of sharp 90s styling, rally-inspired drivetrain options, and the performance history tied to DSM cars. You can learn more from this model overview on Wikipedia.
